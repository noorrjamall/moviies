My Top Five Favorite Noovies

Blue Velvet

A neo-noir mystery film directed by David Lynch. It explores the dark underbelly of a seemingly idyllic suburban town.

A Clockwork Orange

Stanley Kubrick’s dystopian crime film based on Anthony Burgess's novel, exploring themes of free will and the nature of violence.

The French Dispatch

Wes Anderson’s love letter to journalism, this film features an anthology of stories from the final issue of an American magazine published in a fictional French city.

Nocturnal Animals

Directed by Tom Ford, this psychological thriller follows a woman who is haunted by her ex-husband’s novel, which seems to mirror her own life.

Atonement

An adaptation of Ian McEwan’s novel, this period drama tells the story of a young girl’s false accusation that changes the lives of two lovers forever.
